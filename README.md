# HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard – Power BI

## 📊 Project Overview
The **HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard** is a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze employee attrition patterns within an organization.  
It helps HR teams and management understand **why employees leave**, **which departments are most affected**, and **what factors influence attrition**.

This dashboard converts raw HR data into **actionable insights** using visual analytics and key performance indicators (KPIs).

---

## 🎯 Objectives
The main goals of this dashboard are:

- Monitor overall **employee attrition rate**
- Identify **departments with highest attrition**
- Analyze attrition by **age, gender, job role, and salary**
- Discover patterns that contribute to employee turnover
- Support **data-driven HR decision making**

---

## 📁 Dataset Information
The dataset used in this project contains employee-related information such as:

- Employee ID  
- Age  
- Gender  
- Department  
- Job Role  
- Salary  
- Years at Company  
- Job Satisfaction  
- Attrition Status (Yes/No)  

The dataset is transformed in Power BI to create calculated columns and KPIs.

---

## ⚙️ Data Transformation
Data preprocessing was performed using **Power Query** in Power BI.

Steps performed:

1. Loaded dataset into Power BI  
2. Cleaned missing or inconsistent values  
3. Created a new **Attrition Count column**  
4. Converted categorical fields where required  
5. Structured the data for visualization  

---

## 📈 Key KPIs in Dashboard

### 1️⃣ Attrition Count
Displays the total number of employees who left the company.

### 2️⃣ Attrition Rate
Percentage of employees who have left relative to total employees.

### 3️⃣ Total Employees
Shows the overall workforce size.

### 4️⃣ Average Salary
Displays the average salary of employees.

### 5️⃣ Average Years at Company
Indicates employee tenure.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

The dashboard includes the following visual components:

- **Attrition by Department**  
  Bar chart showing which departments have the highest attrition.

- **Attrition by Job Role**  
  Helps identify roles with high employee turnover.

- **Attrition by Age Group**  
  Shows which age groups leave the company most frequently.

- **Attrition by Gender**  
  Compares attrition between male and female employees.

- **Salary vs Attrition**  
  Analyzes whether salary impacts employee turnover.

- **Years at Company vs Attrition**  
  Shows if new or experienced employees leave more often.

### Filters (Slicers)

- Department  
- Gender  
- Job Role  
- Age Group  

These allow dynamic exploration of the data.
